Welcome to My Research

About Me

I am an economics researcher with a focus on macroeconomics, econometrics, and policy analysis. My work spans crisis forecasting, economic convergence, and the behavioral aspects of economic decision-making. I employ both traditional econometric methods and modern computational approaches to understand complex economic phenomena.

Research Interests

Macroeconomic Forecasting

Crisis prediction models and economic convergence analysis

Policy Analysis

Education policy impacts and gender disparity studies

Behavioral Economics

Consumer behavior in technology adoption and decision-making

Applied Econometrics

Causal inference and regression discontinuity designs
